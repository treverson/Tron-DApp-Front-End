Who We Help

Health Port is a patient-centric healthcare information system that will bring patients a portable, interoperable, decentralized, integrated system for managing their personal electronic health records.

Patients will control access to their records via private key and will be the ultimate gatekeeper to their medical data via blockchain-based storage. As a result, private health data is secured and protected from data breaches that have been commonplace over recent years. Health Port is a HIPAA compliant, borderless, open platform that will put the power to control health data into patient's hand.

Patients

Instantly access your health information and improve the quality of medical care you receive. You are in full control of your health record and who may access it.

Providers

Via patient, gain instant access to relevant medical history regardless of where previous care was provided.

Healthcare Organizations

Improve patient outcomes and the ability of providers to provide quality care.

Why Health Port?

Improved Patient Care Outcomes

Improved Patient Care Outcomes

Health Port leverages current patient data being generated through software that is already implemented by healthcare providers, giving patients the power to be the gatekeeper of their own electronic health record (EHR). Through Health Port, patient's own the control and storage of their electronic health record via decentralized storage on the blockchain. The result is a consolidated and integrated medical record that is always available with access controlled by the patient. This fundamental shift will extract immense value from currently implemented EHR systems, ultimately benefiting patient care through improved outcomes. Additionally, providers will be left with a more accurate picture of a specific patient's health, providing them with the comprehensive information they need in order to provide better care to patients.

Disruptive Innovation

Disruptive Innovation

With vast amounts of data already being captured in existing EHR implementations, the timing is optimal for leveraging and consolidating existing data for patient benefit. Health Port will disrupt the global EHR market by removing the barriers currently in place by intermediaries, ultimately allowing patients open access to their complete personal health records.
